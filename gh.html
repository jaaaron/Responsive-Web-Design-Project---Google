The Beginning Of Google

The search engine that changed the world

Co-founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin circia 2002

Here's the beginning history of Google:
  • September 15, 1997 - google.com was registered.
  • September 4, 1998 - Google becomes formally incorporated in Menlo Park, California.
  • August 31, 1999 - Their first patent titled "Watermarking system and methodology for digital
    multimedia content" under their assignee name "Google Inc."
  • October 23, 2000 - Google begins attracting a large following of internet users and begins selling
    advertisements through text-based keywords
  • August 19, 2004 - Google moves into its official headquarters in Mountain View, California. (Googleplex)
  • February 8, 2005 - Google Maps is launched.
  • March 9, 2006 - Google Docs, Google Sheets, and Google Slides is launched.
  • October 9, 2006 - Google acquires Youtube for $1.65 billion.
    • September 2, 2008 - Google launches its freeware web browser.
